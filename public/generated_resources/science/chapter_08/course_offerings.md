## NCERT Class 10 Science: Heredity

### Course Description

This comprehensive course delves into the fascinating world of Heredity, exploring the fundamental principles that govern how traits are passed from parents to offspring. Building upon basic biological concepts, students will embark on a journey to understand the mechanisms of inheritance, the role of genes and chromosomes, and the impact of genetic variation. Through engaging content and interactive learning, this course aims to demystify the science of heredity and equip students with a solid foundation for understanding genetics and its implications.

### Learning Objectives

Upon successful completion of this course, students will be able to:

*   Explain the basic concepts of heredity, including genes, alleles, and traits.
*   Describe Mendel's laws of inheritance (Law of Segregation and Law of Independent Assortment) and apply them to solve simple genetic problems.
*   Differentiate between dominant and recessive alleles and understand their expression in phenotypes.
*   Explain the structure and function of chromosomes and their role in heredity.
*   Understand the concept of sex determination and its genetic basis.
*   Appreciate the importance of genetic variation and its role in evolution.

### Target Audience

This course is designed for students in **NCERT Class 10** as per the prescribed syllabus. It is also suitable for any student seeking to strengthen their understanding of fundamental genetic principles.

### Prerequisites

*   Basic understanding of cell structure and function.
*   Familiarity with the concept of reproduction in living organisms.

### Assessment Plan

*   **Quizzes (30%):** Short quizzes at the end of each module to assess immediate comprehension of key concepts.
*   **Problem-Solving Assignments (40%):** Assignments focusing on applying Mendel's laws and Punnett Square to solve genetic crosses and scenarios.
*   **End-of-Course Examination (30%):** A comprehensive examination covering all modules, including theoretical questions and problem-solving.
