## Question Bank: The Human Eye and Colourful World (Easy Level)

This question bank focuses on basic recall and understanding of the key concepts presented in the chapter.

---

### Section 1: Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

**Instructions:** Choose the correct option for each question.

1.  Which part of the eye forms an image on a light-sensitive screen?
    a) Cornea
    b) Iris
    c) Lens
    d) Retina

2.  The light-sensitive screen in the human eye is called:
    a) Iris
    b) Pupil
    c) Retina
    d) Cornea

3.  What is the dark muscular diaphragm that controls the size of the pupil?
    a) Lens
    b) Retina
    c) Cornea
    d) Iris

4.  The pupil regulates the amount of ______ entering the eye.
    a) Sound
    b) Light
    c) Heat
    d) Smell

5.  The eye lens forms an ______ image of the object on the retina.
    a) Real and erect
    b) Virtual and erect
    c) Real and inverted
    d) Virtual and inverted

6.  What is the ability of the eye lens to adjust its focal length called?
    a) Refraction
    b) Accommodation
    c) Reflection
    d) Dispersion

7.  For a normal eye, the far point is at:
    a) 25 cm
    b) 0 cm
    c) Infinity
    d) 1 meter

8.  Myopia is also known as:
    a) Far-sightedness
    b) Near-sightedness
    c) Old age sight
    d) Colour blindness

9.  In myopia, the image is formed ______ the retina.
    a) In front of
    b) Behind
    c) On
    d) Far away from

10. Which type of lens is used to correct myopia?
    a) Convex lens
    b) Concave lens
    c) Cylindrical lens
    d) Biconvex lens
