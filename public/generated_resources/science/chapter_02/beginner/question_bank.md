## Acids, Bases and Salts - Easy Question Bank

**Instructions:** Answer all questions.

### Section 1: Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) - 1 Mark Each

1.  Acids are ____ in taste.
    a) Bitter
    b) Sweet
    c) Sour
    d) Salty

2.  Bases are ____ in taste.
    a) Sour
    b) Bitter
    c) Sweet
    d) Sour and Bitter

3.  Which of the following is a natural indicator?
    a) Methyl orange
    b) Phenolphthalein
    c) Litmus
    d) Soap

4.  Litmus solution is extracted from:
    a) Turmeric
    b) Lichen
    c) Rose petals
    d) Red cabbage

5.  An acid turns blue litmus paper to:
    a) Blue
    b) Red
    c) Purple
    d) Green

6.  A base turns red litmus paper to:
    a) Red
    b) Blue
    c) Yellow
    d) Orange

7.  When an acid reacts with a metal, it produces hydrogen gas and a:
    a) Base
    b) Salt
    c) Water
    d) Carbon dioxide

8.  Metal carbonates react with acids to produce:
    a) Oxygen gas
    b) Hydrogen gas
    c) Carbon dioxide gas
    d) Nitrogen gas

9.  Which of the following is a base?
    a) Lemon juice
    b) Vinegar
    c) Baking soda solution
    d) Curd

10. Which of the following is an acid?
    a) Soapy water
    b) Toothpaste
    c) Lemon juice
    d) Baking soda solution
