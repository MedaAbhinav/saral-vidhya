      Definition: Substances that help identify acidic or basic nature by changing color or odour.
      Types of Indicators

          Litmus

              Acidic Solution: Blue Litmus → Red
              Basic Solution: Red Litmus → Blue
              Neutral Solution: Purple
          Turmeric
            Properties:
              Basic Medium: Reddish-brown (e.g., soap on curry stain)

            Red cabbage leaves

        2. Synthetic Indicators
          Phenolphthalein
          Methyl Orange
        3. Olfactory Indicators (Activity 2.2)
          Definition: Substances whose odour changes in acidic or basic media.

            Onion (finely chopped)
