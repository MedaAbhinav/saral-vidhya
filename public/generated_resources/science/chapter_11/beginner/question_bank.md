## NCERT Class 10 Science: Electricity - Easy Level Question Bank

### 1. Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) - 1 Mark Each

1.  What is the main form of energy that electricity represents?
    a) Light energy
    b) Heat energy
    c) Controllable and convenient energy
    d) Nuclear energy

2.  What flows through a conductor when we say there is an electric current?
    a) Heat
    b) Light
    c) Electric charge
    d) Sound

3.  What does a switch do in an electric circuit?
    a) It produces electric current.
    b) It makes a conducting link between the cell and the bulb.
    c) It stops the flow of heat.
    d) It makes the bulb brighter.

4.  What is a continuous and closed path of an electric current called?
    a) Electric conductor
    b) Electric switch
    c) Electric circuit
    d) Electric cell

5.  What is the SI unit of electric charge?
    a) Ampere (A)
    b) Volt (V)
    c) Coulomb (C)
    d) Joule (J)

6.  Who is the SI unit of electric current, the Ampere (A), named after?
    a) Alessandro Volta
    b) Andre-Marie Ampere
    c) Isaac Newton
    d) Albert Einstein

7.  What instrument measures electric current in a circuit?
    a) Voltmeter
    b) Ammeter
    c) Rheostat
    d) Switch

8.  How is an ammeter always connected in a circuit?
    a) In parallel
    b) In series
    c) In the middle
    d) Independently

9.  What is the SI unit of electric potential difference?
    a) Ampere (A)
    b) Coulomb (C)
    c) Watt (W)
    d) Volt (V)

10. What instrument is used to measure the potential difference between two points?
    a) Ammeter
    b) Ohmmeter
    c) Voltmeter
    d) Galvanometer
