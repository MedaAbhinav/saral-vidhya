### Section 1: Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) - 1 Mark Each

Choose the correct option for each question.

1.  What makes things visible to us?
    a) Darkness
    b) Sound
    c) Reflected light
    d) Heat

2.  Light travels in which kind of path?
    a) Curved path
    b) Straight line
    c) Zigzag path
    d) Random path

3.  A highly polished surface, like a mirror, does what to light falling on it?
    a) Absorbs most of it
    b) Transmits most of it
    c) Reflects most of it
    d) Bends most of it

4.  Which of the following is a law of reflection?
    a) The angle of incidence is half the angle of reflection.
    b) The incident ray and reflected ray are perpendicular.
    c) The angle of incidence is equal to the angle of reflection.
    d) The normal is parallel to the mirror.

5.  What type of image is formed by a plane mirror?
    a) Real and inverted
    b) Virtual and erect
    c) Real and erect
    d) Virtual and inverted

6.  A curved mirror whose reflecting surface is curved inwards is called:
    a) Convex mirror
    b) Plane mirror
    c) Concave mirror
    d) Spherical mirror

7.  A curved mirror whose reflecting surface is curved outwards is called:
    a) Concave mirror
    b) Convex mirror
    c) Plane mirror
    d) Spherical mirror

8.  The center of the reflecting surface of a spherical mirror is called the:
    a) Focus
    b) Center of curvature
    c) Pole
    d) Principal axis

9.  The distance from the pole to the principal focus is called:
    a) Radius of curvature
    b) Diameter
    c) Focal length
    d) Aperture

10. The straight line passing through the pole and center of curvature of a spherical mirror is called the:
    a) Normal
    b) Tangent
    c) Principal axis
    d) Optical axis
