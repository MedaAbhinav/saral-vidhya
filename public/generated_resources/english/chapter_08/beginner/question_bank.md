    b) Sage
    c) Prince

3.  How long did Siddhartha Gautama wander in search of enlightenment?
    a) Five years
    b) Seven years

    a) Wisdom Tree

    a) Kapilavastu

    a) A handful of rice

    a) Medicine can cure everything.

#### Answer Key

#### 1. Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
1.  c) Prince
2.  c) Twenty-five
3.  b) Seven years
4.  c) Bodhi Tree
5.  c) Benares
6.  b) Her only son had died.
7.  c) Medicine for her child
8.  b) A handful of mustard-seed
9.  b) No one had ever lost a child, husband, parent or friend.
10. b) Death is common to all.

#### 2. Very Short Answer Questions (VSA)
1.  His birth name was Siddhartha Gautama.
2.  A sick man and an aged man. (Other valid answers: a funeral procession, a monk begging for alms)
3.  The word 'Buddha' means 'The Awakened' or 'The Enlightened'.
4.  A man she met on her way directed her to the Buddha.
5.  According to the Buddha, death is the inevitable fate of all mortals in this world.

#### 3. Fill in the Blanks
1.  scriptures
2.  sufferings
3.  peepal
4.  Benares
5.  selfish
