Imagine a lady named Kisa Gotami whose little boy died. She was very, very sad and wanted someone to bring him back.

She went to Buddha, who asked her to find mustard seeds from a house where *no one* had ever died. Kisa Gotami searched everywhere but couldn't find such a house! Everyone had lost someone.

She then understood that death happens to everyone, always. Buddha taught her that getting too sad and crying won't change anything. We must accept that losing loved ones is a normal part of life.
