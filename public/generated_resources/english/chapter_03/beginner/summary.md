Hey! This chapter has two cool stories about flying!

First, there's a baby seagull who is super scared to fly. His family pushes him, and he finally jumps, learns to flap his wings, and flies on his own! He becomes brave.

Then, there's a pilot who flies into a huge, scary storm. His plane stops working, and he's lost. But a mysterious black plane appears and guides him safely out of the storm.

Both stories teach us that even when things are scary, we can be brave and find help to get through tough times.
