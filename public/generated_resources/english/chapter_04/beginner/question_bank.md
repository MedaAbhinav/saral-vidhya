### NCERT Class 10 English: "From the Diary of Anne Frank" - Question Bank

#### 1. Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) - (1 mark each)

Choose the correct option for each question.

1.  Anne Frank was a German-born ______ girl.
    a) Christian
    b) Jewish
    c) Muslim
    d) Buddhist

2.  During which major historical event did Anne Frank write her diary?
    a) World War I
    b) The Cold War
    c) World War II
    d) The Renaissance

3.  Where did Anne Frank and her family go into hiding?
    a) In a friend's house
    b) In a concentration camp
    c) In her father Otto Frank’s office building
    d) In a secret bunker

4.  Who was the only member of the Frank family's hiding group to survive the war?
    a) Anne Frank
    b) Margot Frank
    c) Edith Frank
    d) Otto Frank

5.  What was Anne Frank's diary published as in English?
    a) A Girl's Secret Life
    b) The Diary of a Young Girl
    c) Anne's Hidden Story
    d) My Secret Friend

6.  On what occasion did Anne Frank receive her diary?
    a) Her birthday
    b) Christmas
    c) Her graduation
    d) New Year's Eve

7.  The original diary of Anne Frank was written in which language?
    a) English
    b) German
    c) Dutch
    d) French

8.  What special name did Anne Frank give to her diary?
    a) Pen Pal
    b) Kitty
    c) Friend
    d) Confidante

9.  Who was Anne Frank's older sister?
    a) Kitty
    b) Edith
    c) Margot
    d) Mrs. Kuperus

10. What made the students "quaking in its boots" on 20 June 1942?
    a) A surprise test
    b) The annual sports day
    c) A meeting where teachers decide who moves to the next class
    d) A visit from a famous person

#### 2. Very Short Answer Questions (1 mark each)

Answer each question in one line.

1.  What was Anne Frank's full name?
2.  What did Anne Frank die of in Bergen-Belsen?
3.  Why did Anne Frank feel a great need to write in a diary?
4.  Who was Anne Frank's headmistress and sixth-form teacher?
5.  What significant event happened to Anne's grandmother in January 1942?

#### 3. Fill in the Blanks (1 mark each)

Complete the following sentences with the correct word(s).

1.  Anne Frank's family went into hiding in ______________ in July 1942.
2.  Anne Frank's father, ______________, returned to Amsterdam after the war.
3.  The diary chronicles events from 12 June 1942 until its final entry of 1 August ______________.
4.  Anne thought that "__________ has more patience than people."
5.  Anne's sister, Margot, was born in __________ in 1926.
