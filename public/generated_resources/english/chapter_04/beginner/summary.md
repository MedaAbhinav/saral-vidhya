This story is about Anne Frank, a young Jewish girl who had to hide with her family during World War II because of bad people called Nazis.

She started writing in a diary on her thirteenth birthday. She felt lonely and didn't have a "true friend" to talk to, so she treated her diary like one, sharing all her thoughts and feelings.

Sadly, Anne died, but her father saved her diary. It became a very famous book that tells her important story of life in hiding to the whole world.
